Intro

Modding is a great way to breath new life into an old game or fix and overhaul a game's outdating/poorly inplimated features

Mods and how to get them

A great game to mod is fallout 4 Get a Nexus account and download their mod manager Vortex, this is a mod manager with a few clicks you can donload a mod just make sure you check the Nexus page to check compatability. The mod manager creates a mod folder in the games directory and unzips the zipped mod files then it puts them into the mod folder for the game to access them.

Zipped folders and how to get mods that are not compatible with Nexus

The mods are zipped to keep downloads small if you notice Nexus limits non-premium members to 2mbps downloads but with the small files it should only take a short time. In order to extract or unzip the ziped files get the newest versions Winrar or 7zip I recommend 7zip for its ease of use. Now download Fallout 4 Script extender and right click you will see 7zip hover on it and click extract. Now follow the directions on the Faloout 4 script extenders Nexus page
